Why HCBS Matters

Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS) are the foundation of California's commitment to supporting individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities in their own communities.

These programs enable people to live independently, find meaningful employment, build relationships, and contribute to their communities ‚Äî rather than being isolated in institutional settings.

When people receive care and support in their own communities, everyone benefits.
