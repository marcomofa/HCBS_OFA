Understanding Home & Community-Based Services (HCBS) in California

HCBS programs help people with intellectual and developmental disabilities live, work, and belong in their communities. Learn how these programs support inclusion, independence, and opportunity across California.

What Are HCBS?

Supporting independence and community inclusion for all Californians

Overview

Home & Community-Based Services (HCBS) offer individualized supports delivered in homes and communities rather than institutions. Services are tailored to personal goals so people can build skills, relationships, and independence close to family and community.

Examples include employment services, independent living skills, community engagement, personal assistance, assistive technology, transportation, and caregiver education.

When people receive supports in their own communities, everyone benefits.
